Thai Basil Chicken

Description

Little bit of chicken, little bit of Thai Basil, not really too much more to explain, a simple but classic meal served over rice. Can be modified depending on spice preference, but I usually make it without chillies.

Ingredients:

  • Three chicken breasts
  • Rice
  • One large onion
  • Garlic
  • Soy Sauce
  • Fish Sauce
  • Chicken Broth
  • Thai Basil
  • Spinach
  • Thai Green Chilis
  • Brown Sugar

Steps:

  1. Start cooking 1 and half cups of rice, or however much you think you can eat in two meals
  2. Slice chicken into little cubes and throw it into a pan on high heat (with oil in it in case you needed to know that)
  3. While chicken is cooking, throw in your whole onion (diced)
  4. In a separate bowl, mix up your sauce, around 2 teaspoons of fish sauce, 1/3 of a cup soy sauce, 3/4 cup chicken broth, ~6 cloves of diced garlic, and two tablespoons of brown sugar
  5. Add sauce to pan, lower heat a bit
  6. Let sauce simmer down to the point where it is nice and viscous, then turn off heat and add in a ludicrous amount of spinach and Basil
  7. At this point if you feeling spicy with it and some sliced green chillies
  8. Throw that stuff over some rice and consume
