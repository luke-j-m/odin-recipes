Shepherd's Pie

Description

What an absolutely classic meal, based on Gordon Ramsey's mothers Shepherd's pie, this is my recollection of the classic fall/winter time dish. Prepare yourself for a legendary experience of one the most hearty and flavourfull meals that can possibly be made.

Ingredients:

  • Ground Turkey or Ground Beef
  • Shallots
  • Carrots
  • Worcestershire Sauce
  • Red Wine
  • Thyme
  • Rosemary
  • Oregano
  • Herbs De Provence
  • Potatoes
  • Butter
  • Milk
  • Parmesan Cheese

Steps:

  1. Heat pan on medium-high heat, add olive oil and put in ground turkey/beef when oil is hot
  2. Add in herbs after meat is mostly cooked, while meat is cooking, cut up shallots and carrots
  3. Quickly quarter the potatoes and put them in a pot with ROOM TEMPERATURE water, then boil
  4. Create a well in the meat and pour some more oil in, then add shallots and when shallots are sweating add carrots
  5. Once shallots are nicely cooked, add in a ridiculous amount of Worcestershire sauce, seriously theres no measurements around here
  6. Add in a good amount of red wine but make sure you have some left to drink with dinner, lower heat so meat and veggies are simmering, getting nice and tasty.
  7. Preheat oven to 350
  8. At this point you should be able to stick a fork through the potatoes with minimal effort, remove water and add butter and milk to potatoes and mash
  9. Pour meat mixture out of pan into glass dish or something, use spatual to layer mash potatoes on top
  10. Put Pie into oven for around 10 minutes, with a couple minutes left, sprinkle Parmesan cheese on top
  11. Try not to get too excited, pour yourself a glass of wine and enjoy
